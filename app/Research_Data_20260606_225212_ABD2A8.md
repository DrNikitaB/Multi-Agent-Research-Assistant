### Capital of Delhi

Based on the provided sources, the relationship between Delhi and its capital is defined as follows:

*   **New Delhi as the Capital:** New Delhi is the capital of India [2]. It is a small part of the larger city of Delhi [2].
*   **National Capital Territory (NCT):** New Delhi and Delhi are combined to form the National Capital Territory of Delhi [2]. New Delhi is a part of this territory [2].
*   **Administrative Role:** New Delhi serves as the location from which the central government of India regulates the country's functioning [2]. It houses key institutions, including the Parliament of India, the Supreme Court of India, and the Rashtrapati Bhavan [2].
*   **Historical Context:** New Delhi was established in 1911 and was inaugurated on 13 February 1931 [2]. It was designed by Edwin Lutyens and Herbert Baker [2].

In summary, while "Delhi" refers to the broader megacity and union territory, **New Delhi** is the specific city that serves as the capital of India and is located within the National Capital Territory of Delhi [1, 2].
